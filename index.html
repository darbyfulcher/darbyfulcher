About Darby

Darby Fulcher is a performer, originally from San Antonio, TX, who now calls New York City home! After training at a competition studio growing up, Darby made the move to NYC for undergrad, where she earned her Commercial Dance degree and gradduated from the honors program at Pace School of the Performing Arts. She has gone on to perform on Holland America Line's Eurodam with RWS Entertainment Group, with Brooklyn based contemporary company Bloodline Dance Theatre in "Theories & Apologies", in an immersive rendition of The Tempest with The Secret Theatre in Long Island City, in a workshop production of Fragments, produced by UptownWorks and performed at the Dixon Place, with contemporary company Ninja Ballet in "Freedom", and, most recently and very joyously, in West Side Story with The Lexington Theatre Co. in Kentucky. When she's not performing, you can find Darby teaching dance and barre classes, traveling, creating websites (like this one!), and exploring all that New York City has to offer- especially the best food spots!
